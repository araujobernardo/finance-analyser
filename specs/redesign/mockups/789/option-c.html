Option C — Month Summary Rows

Instead of a vertical bar chart, each month gets a compact horizontal row with two track bars (income teal, expenses red). The track bar widths are proportional to the max value across all months. Each bar shows its exact NZD amount on the right. A net badge (+/- saved) appears below each row's bars.

The current month row has a subtle teal-tinted background. Hover any row for a light background highlight. No separate chart area is needed — the list IS the chart.

Tradeoff: Horizontal bars are less immediately comparable than vertical grouped bars. Month-over-month trend direction is harder to read at a glance than with vertical bars.
